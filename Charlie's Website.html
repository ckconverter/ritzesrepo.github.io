Society of the Future

Communism is often portrayed as a dangerous or scary idea, but we're here to tell you that's not true. In fact, communism is better than fascism and capitalism in many ways.

Communism was an idea created by Karl Marx and enacted by Vladimir Lenin. Since then communist nations have defeated capitalist ones in literally every way, from wars, to technology, to happiness of the people. As for fascism, that died when the communists destroyed them in World War Two.
